Where do I find my token?
  • Running locally: it's printed in your terminal when IronClaw starts (or whatever you set GATEWAY_AUTH_TOKEN to).
  • Hosted on NEAR AI Cloud: open your instance from agent.near.ai and you're signed in automatically — no token needed.
  • Need a token for scripts or the API? Sign in, open the account menu, and create one under "API tokens".
