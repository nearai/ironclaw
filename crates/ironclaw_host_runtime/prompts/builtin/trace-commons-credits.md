# Trace Commons credits

Reports the current user's Trace Commons credit state: pending and final credit balance, submission counts, and recent credit explanations. Call this when the user asks how their trace contribution credits are doing, their balance, or whether credits are accruing. Read-only; reflects the local view as of the last sync (the authoritative ledger is server-side). Final credit can still change after privacy review, replay/eval, duplicate checks, and downstream utility scoring — don't present pending credit as guaranteed.
